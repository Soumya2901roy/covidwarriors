COVID-19 Plasma donors - INDIA

This website is to help COVID-19 patients to connect them with recovered people who want to donate their convalescent plasma*

*Plasma treatment is still under testing and research, and it isn't 100% effective.


How does it work?

For Active COVID-19 Patients

Convalescent plasma is the liquid part of blood that is collected from patients who have recovered from the novel coronavirus disease, and their antibodies can help fight the infection. You can use the MAP(to be populated once we have enough data) to find the nearest donor and get in touch to go to the nearest plasma donation center.For now to find the list of donors please click the below button

Donors!

People who have fully recovered from COVID-19 for at least two weeks are encouraged to consider donating plasma, which may help save the lives of other patients. You can add your information to our database, and only your First name, Blood type and Contact number will be Visible.

Read this before Donating plasma:
1. Only 14 days after symptoms disapper and negative swab result, Or 28 days after symptoms are gone.
2. You have positive RT-PCR report for COVID-19.
3. You cannot donate if you are pregnant.
4. You do not have chronic diseases, especially insulin diabetics.
5. You did not have tattoos or piercings within the last 4 months.
6. You have valid identification and a permanent address.
7. Only if are above 18 years and weigh at least 50 KGs.
